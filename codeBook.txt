"subject"  
        List of subject in the experiment
                01...30 .Integer Id of each subject
          
"activity" 
        Activity performed by each subject
                1 .Walking
                2 .Walking_Upstairs
                3 .Walking_Downstairs
                4 .Sitting
                5 .Standing
                6 .Laying
                


The following variables are named based on the following rules:
    - 't' stands for time domain signals
    - 'f' stands for Fast Fourier Transform
    - 'Acc' stands for accelerometer signals
    - 'Gyro' stands for gyroscope signals
    - 'Jerk' stands for Jerk signals (obtained by body linear acceleration and angular velocity derived in time)
    - 'Freq' stands for frequency domain signals
    - 'Mag' stands for magnitude of the signals
    - 'mean()' Mean value
    - 'std()' Standard deviation
    - 'angle()' Angle between to vectors
    - 'X','Y','Z' are the 3-axial measurments
